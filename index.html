Purpose

Perla, we both share a dream to contribute significantly in a positive way to the world of healthcare. I believe we can focus our efforts on providing the requisite healthcare by finding a way to incorporate natural remedies into medical treatment based on evidence-based principles. Our objective would be to decrease reliance on biosynthesized chemicals in the lab, but seeing how best we can harness the power of natural compounds in plants to produce effective less costly remedies which will be an adjunct in current best-practice healthcare.
